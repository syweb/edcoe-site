Departments and Services

In partnership with students, families and the community, EDCOE provides leadership with a wide variety of programs and services to support students as citizens of the future.

EDCOE provides services, administration and infrastructure as a partner with parents for a variety of education programs supporting:

  • Young Children
  • School Age Children
  • Alternative Programs
  • Students with Disabilities
  • Student Enrichment Activities

In addition, EDCOE provides additional support and services in the area of:

  • District Fiscal Oversight
  • Business Services
  • Information Technology
  • Personnel Services
  • Facilities, Maintenance and Operations
  • Developer Fee Collections
  • Print Shop Services
  • Curriculum, Instruction and Accountability
  • Professional Development and Training
  • Emergency Preparedness
