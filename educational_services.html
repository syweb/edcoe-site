Educational Services

Educational Services is dedicated to providing educational excellence to prepare every student for success in their individual role to meet the challenges of tomorrow’s society.

Preparing for the 21st Century

The foundation for success in the 21st century will be the ability to think critically, solve problems creatively, and to communicate and collaborate effectively. Common Core State Standards will provide opportunities for students to acquire and perfect these “21st century skills” so they can be prepared to succeed in higher education and compete in the global economy.

Ed comes to the El Dorado County Office of Education from the Sacramento City Unified School District, where he currently works as Interim Chief of Staff. Prior to that time, he served as the Superintendent, Principal, and Director of Strategic Partnerships with St. Hope Public Schools in Sacramento. Under Ed’s leadership as the Principal of Sacramento High, the school demonstrated significant academic growth and a four- year college acceptance rate that increased from 25% to 75%. He obtained his Doctorate of Education in Educational Leadership from the University of California, Davis, in March 2011, and his Master’s Degree in Social Work in 1998. He was recently accepted into the Chief Business Officials Mentor Training Program.

Ed firmly believes that an excellent education is the most critical lever for all communities to help children fulfill their potential. Ed and his wife have three children, and he enjoys spending time with family and friends, reading, and long distance running.
