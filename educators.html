For Educators

EDCOE provides leadership for the changing needs of educators in supporting educational excellence. Professional education, innovation, information and training is provided on many levels to meet the emerging Common Core Standards, evolving technologies and challenges of preparing each and every student for life success.

Training, information and resources provided cover:

  • Beginning Teacher Support
  • Common Core Standards
  • Early Childhood & Special Needs Training
  • English Learners
  • Emerging Technologies
  • Instructional Practice
  • Professional Growth and Leadership
  • School Readiness
