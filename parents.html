Parents

Parents and families play an important role in student success. EDCOE provides these resources to strengthen the bond between home and school.

EDCOE provides services, administration and infrastructure as a partner with parents for a variety of education programs. Included in our service offerings are child development programs for young children 0-5 years of age which promote the well-being of the whole child to prepare them for school. For school age children, EDCOE operates a variety of alternative programs for student enrichment and for students with special needs. In addition to the school day, services include extended day childcare providing high quality educational activities.
