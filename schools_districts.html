El Dorado County Districts and Schools

EDCOE partners with the county's 15 districts and their schools to serve over 28,900 students providing strong foundations that prepare students for future success.

The school districts and community college districts are independent from EDCOE in that they are governed by their locally elected Boards of Trustees. The links below are provided by them as a resource for you.
